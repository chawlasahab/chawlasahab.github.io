Pizza Crafting

Authentic Neapolitan Pizza

My perfected recipe after 3 years of experimentation

Ingredients

  • 00 Flour - 500g
  • Water - 325ml (65% hydration)
  • Sea Salt - 10g
  • Fresh Yeast - 1g
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Basil Leaves
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Process

  1. Mix flour, water, salt and yeast
  2. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth
  3. Bulk ferment at room temp for 2 hours
  4. Divide into 250g balls and refrigerate for 48h
  5. Bring to room temp 4 hours before baking
  6. Stretch by hand, top minimally
  7. Bake at 900°F for 60-90 seconds
